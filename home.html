About Us

Mayuresh Super Specialty Hospital is situated in Navi Mumbai, a twin city of Mumbai. It is a 35 bedded Super Specialty Hospital, centrally located on Vashi Turbhe Road, easily accessible and reachable from Mumbai and Suburban Mumbai, it is in close proximity to Chembur & Thane City.

The Hospital is Centrally Air-conditioned and maintains utmost hygiene, ensuring safety and quality service to the patients. The team at the Hospital is friendly and caring, the Consultants, Staff Nurses, Aaya, Maushi, Paramedics all strive hard to fulfill the requirement of the patients and arrange all facilities under one roof.
